Hawaii

A trip to the Hawaiian islands is a tropical paradise. Scenes and activities that are experiences of a lifetime. Anywhere you go theres so much to do and beautiful places to be seen. They have a very unique and rich culture that can be enjoyed by anyone on a visit to their islands. Dont forget to go to an authentic luau while in Hawaii.

Scotland

Scotland is filled with something for everyone. With its beautiful landscapes, historical castles, friendly locals, great food and so much more; there's so many options. Also, go take a look at Lock-Ness to see if you can spot Nessie. Scotland has some of the most charismatic locals you'll meet, they'll be glad to tell you the best their homeland has to offer and they also know how to party.

Los Angeles

The famous Los Angeles, city of angels, is a busy destination in the United States. Nearby are many different places to go: incliding Disneyland, Six Flags Magic Mountain, many different beaches on the coast, Hollywood and so much more. It has a lot to offer and a vibrant night life as well. Filled with many amazing restaraunts and might possibly see a movie star.

Alaska

A bit more on the chillier side of the world, but that means many different scenes to see and activities to do. Be able to see gigantic glaciers and iceburgs scattered on the ocean, bald eagles throughout the forest, and beautiful snow-crested mountains and waterfalls. If the weather is clear enough, you may also be able to see the Northern Lights.

Pandora
